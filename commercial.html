Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our commercial moving services

Have questions? We're here to help!

Our team is available to answer all your moving questions

Can you move after hours or on weekends?

Yes. We regularly schedule evening and weekend moves to minimize business disruption and reduce downtime for your team.

How do you protect elevators and common areas?

We coordinate with building management and use floor runners, door guards, and protective pads as required to protect all common areas.

Do you handle IT equipment?

Yes, we protect and transport computers, monitors, and peripherals with proper padding. For server racks and specialized equipment, we'll create a custom plan.
