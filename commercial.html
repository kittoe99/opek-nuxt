What’s included with our commercial moves

Office moves require tight coordination. We label, protect, and stage items at destination so teams are productive on day one.

  • • Color‑coded labels by department, floor, and room
  • • IT and electronics protection with clean pads and wrap
  • • Conference tables and fixtures disassembly/reassembly
  • • Elevator, dock, and hallway protection where required
  • • Placement by plan at destination for faster setup

Need packing help? We offer full and fragile‑only packing. Try Get Instant Pricing or request a full quote.

Commercial moving prices in Denver

Pricing depends on scope, access, and timeline. Small office moves are often hourly; larger projects are best quoted at a flat rate with a detailed plan.

  • • Hourly for small suites and short distances
  • • Flat‑rate project pricing for multi‑floor or multi‑day moves
  • • After‑hours and weekend options to reduce downtime
