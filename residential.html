Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our residential moving services

Have questions? We're here to help!

Our team is available to answer all your moving questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2–3 weeks in advance for best availability. However, if you have a last-minute move, we'll do our best to accommodate you. Contact us as soon as possible to check availability.

Do you protect floors and doorways?

Absolutely. We use floor runners, door jamb protection, and clean moving pads to protect your home throughout the entire move. This is included in every residential move at no extra charge.

Can you help with packing and unpacking?

Yes! We offer full and partial packing services, plus unpacking and placement where you want items to go. We provide all necessary packing materials including boxes, tape, and protective wrapping.

What about heavy or specialty items?

Please let us know in advance about pianos, safes, large artwork, or other oversized items. We'll bring the appropriate equipment and adjust our team size to handle these items safely and professionally.
