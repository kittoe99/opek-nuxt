Residential Moving FAQ

Answers to common questions about booking, timing, and what’s included.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend 2–3 weeks for best availability. If you have a last‑minute move, we’ll do our best to fit you in.

Do you protect floors and doorways?

+

Yes. We use floor runners, door jamb protection, and clean pads to protect your home throughout the move.

Can you help with packing and unpacking?

+

Absolutely. We offer full and partial packing, plus unpacking and placement where you want items to go.

What about heavy or specialty items?

+

Tell us in advance about pianos, safes, or oversized items. We’ll bring the right equipment and team size.
