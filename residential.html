What’s included with our residential moves

Every home move with Opek includes careful item protection and clear coordination, so moving day feels organized from start to finish.

  • • Floor runners and door jamb protection at pickup and destination
  • • Clean moving pads, plastic wrap, and secure straps for furniture
  • • Basic disassembly and reassembly for beds and tables
  • • Room-by-room placement at your new home
  • • Friendly, background‑checked movers and clear communication

Need packing help? We offer full and partial packing services. For quick estimates, try Get Instant Pricing or request a full quote.

Residential moving prices in Denver

Local residential moves in the Denver area are typically billed hourly based on the size of the crew. Larger or long-distance moves may be quoted at a flat rate after we review details.

  • • Hourly moves: crew size matched to home size and access (stairs, elevator)
  • • Flat-rate quotes: recommended for bigger homes or longer distances
  • • Optional packing: added as hourly or included in a flat-rate plan

Residential Moving FAQ

Answers to common questions about booking, timing, and what’s included.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend 2–3 weeks for best availability. If you have a last‑minute move, we’ll do our best to fit you in.

Do you protect floors and doorways?

+

Yes. We use floor runners, door jamb protection, and clean pads to protect your home throughout the move.

Can you help with packing and unpacking?

+

Absolutely. We offer full and partial packing, plus unpacking and placement where you want items to go.

What about heavy or specialty items?

+

Tell us in advance about pianos, safes, or oversized items. We’ll bring the right equipment and team size.
